Henrique Barbosa

H.BARBOSA is a legal boutique founded by Henrique Barbosa after almost twenty-five years of intense corporate law practice and fifteen years of teaching, the result of a professional career that has always combined practice with solid academic and institutional performance.

Henrique holds a PhD in Commercial Law from USP and is currently the president of IBRADEMP (Brazilian Institute of Coporate Law). He teaches post-graduate courses at INSPER, IBMEC, FGV/RJ, PUC/RS and ILMM, as well being a guest lecturer at the Societário Academy, where he teaches Corporate Litigation, Corporations, Mergers & Acquisitions and Corporate Financing.

In the field of Mergers & Acquisitions, in addition to having attended international programs at prestigious foreign universities (Georgetown Law School and Vanderbilt Law School), throughout his career Henrique has acted as legal advisor in dozens of operations, involving both disposals and acquisitions of shares and fundraising, with a total transaction volume exceeding R$2 billion.

In the arbitration area, Henrique has been consolidating his performance as an arbitrator and member of the lists of some of the most prestigious Arbitration Chambers, accumulating relevant experience by forming tribunals with professionals of unequivocal experience and reputation.

In recent times, he has acted as co-arbitrator or presiding arbitrator in more than twenty arbitrations involving disputes related to contracts and corporate or financial transactions of the most diverse natures, such as corporate dissolutions, compliance with shareholder agreements, liability of partners and/or managers and board members, annulment of resolutions and disputes in M&A transactions in general (e.g., indemnities/compensation, price adjustments, breach of representations and warranties or non-competite clauses and consortium disagreements).
